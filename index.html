January 2025 - Present

Robotics Engineer

Pick-it NV | Leuven, Belgium

Code owner for Motion Planning and Picking components of Pickit production-grade software for industrial manipulators in bin picking applications. Integrated state-of-the-art AI techniques for part detection and point-cloud enhancement using ROS framework. Developed Linux-based A/B swap upgrading mechanism and deployed experimental versions at customer premises for custom development projects.

June 2021 - December 2024

Robotics Engineer

Space Applications Services NV/SA | Zaventem, Belgium

Designed and developed the localization system, autonomous navigation stack, and remote control for LUVMI-X rover for ESA-ESRIC Space Resource Challenge in Luxembourg. Developed embedded software interfaces in C and Python for actuators and payload commanding. Created multi-agent cooperative mapping algorithm for EC Project CoRob-X, demonstrated in lunar analogue in Lanzarote. Developed localization and navigation for UGVs and UAV in EDF projects AIDED and AIDEDeX. Interfaced numerous sensors including RGB cameras, LiDAR, hyperspectral cameras, IMUs, wheel encoders, ground penetrating radar, drills, and spectrometers.

October 2018 - February 2022

M.Sc. in Robotics and Automation Engineering

University of Pisa | Pisa, Italy

Graduated with distinction (110/110 cum laude, GPA: 28/30). Thesis on "Cooperative SLAM for heterogeneous lunar rovers". Relevant coursework included Guidance and Navigation Systems, Control of Robots, Aerospace Robotics, Computational Intelligence, Distributed Robotics Systems, and Underwater Autonomous Vehicles.

July 2018 - October 2018

Bachelor Thesis Internship

STMicroelectronics | Catania, Italy
